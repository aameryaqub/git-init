Agentic AI Architecture

Six Specialized Intelligence Agents

Each agent is purpose-built for a distinct phase of the research and strategy workflow, operating autonomously and in coordination.

Data Acquisition

Research Agent

Autonomously collects and structures market data from diverse public sources, ensuring comprehensive coverage across industries and geographies.

Business Value: Eliminates hours of manual data gathering; ensures no competitor signal is missed.
Competitive Analysis

Competitor Intelligence Agent

Analyzes competitor positioning, pricing strategies, product offerings, and market movements to map the competitive landscape.

Business Value: Real-time awareness of competitive threats and positioning opportunities.
Customer Intelligence

Customer Voice Agent

Processes customer reviews, feedback, and social signals using advanced NLP to extract sentiment, pain points, and unmet needs.

Business Value: Turns unstructured customer feedback into quantified product and service insights.
Market Mapping

Opportunity Discovery Agent

Identifies market white spaces, underserved segments, and high-value opportunities by cross-referencing competitor gaps with customer demand signals.

Business Value: Surfaces growth opportunities that would take weeks to identify manually.
Strategy Generation

Strategy Consultant Agent

Synthesizes all research outputs into coherent strategic recommendations, prioritized by impact and feasibility for the business context.

Business Value: Delivers consulting-quality strategy without the consulting price tag.
Output Delivery

Executive Reporting Agent

Formats all findings into polished, narrative-driven reports with structured sections, key metrics, and clear calls to action for leadership teams.

Business Value: Boardroom-ready deliverables in minutes, not weeks.
